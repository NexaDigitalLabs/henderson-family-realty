Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

Henderson Family Realty ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Adam Henderson, DRE# 02073962, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit hendersonfamilyrealty.com or interact with us through social media platforms including Facebook and Instagram.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and property address submitted through our contact forms or direct outreach.
  • Communication data — messages you send us through our website, email, text, or social media.
  • Social media interactions — when you comment on or interact with our Facebook or Instagram posts, we may receive information associated with your public profile in order to respond to your inquiry.
  • Usage data — general information about how you interact with our website. We do not use invasive tracking or advertising cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to real estate inquiries and provide requested information or guides
  • Send market updates or resources you've requested
  • Provide real estate services and follow up on leads
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements applicable to California licensed real estate professionals

Social Media & Meta Platforms

Henderson Family Realty uses Facebook and Instagram to communicate with clients. When you comment on our posts using keyword triggers (such as "CURB," "VALUE," or "TIMING"), our system may automatically send you a direct message containing the requested information via Instagram or Facebook Messenger.

By interacting with our social media posts, Meta's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service also apply. We do not store or sell data obtained through social media interactions beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only with our brokerage (Coldwell Banker Associated Brokers Realty) as required for real estate transactions, with service providers under confidentiality agreements, or when required by law.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of any personal data we hold, contact us at adam@hfr.homes or call 951-240-8126. We will process your request within 30 days.

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

As a California resident you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.
